# ByteForge Plant Ecommerce Roadmap

This document outlines the strategic phases for developing a specialized multi-vendor platform for plants.

## Project Vision
To empower nursery owners with a nationwide selling platform and robust inventory management, while providing plant enthusiasts with high-quality, care-aware shopping.

---

## 🚀 The "Live" Milestone (Phase 5)
The project is considered "Live" when:
1. Sellers can list specialized plant data (Species, Care Level, Growth Stage).
2. Nationwide shipping configurations are operational.
3. Secure payments (Local gateways) and order lifecycle tracking are complete.

---

## Strategic Phases

### Phase 1: Foundation (Seller Identity)
*   **Goal**: Establish the multi-vendor ecosystem.
*   **Key Features**:
    *   Shop Profile Management (Nursery branding).
    *   Seller/Admin Role Separation.
    *   Taxonomy: Specialized Plant Categories.

### Phase 2: Core Catalog (The Plant Model)
*   **Goal**: Define the specialized data structure for live plants.
*   **Key Features**:
    *   Botanical attributes (Sunlight, Water, pH).
    *   Growth Stage variants (Sapling vs Mature).
    *   Care Library Integration (Links to ByteForge care guides).

### Phase 3: Logistics (Inventory & Media)
*   **Goal**: Real-time stock and visual management.
*   **Key Features**:
    *   Real-time Nursery Stock tracking.
    *   Optimized High-Res Image Gallery for plants.
    *   Inventory alerts for sellers.

### Phase 4: Commerce (Transaction Engine)
*   **Goal**: Enable secure nationwide trading.
*   **Key Features**:
    *   Persistent Cart & Stock Validation.
    *   Local Payment Integration (bKash/SSLCommerz).
    *   Order State Machine: `PREP` -> `SHIPPED` -> `COMPLETE`.

### Phase 5: Go-Live (Smarter Operations)
*   **Goal**: Full operational capacity.
*   **Key Features**:
    *   Seller Performance Dashboards.
    *   Shipping Config Engine (Zones & Care during transit).
    *   Automated Notifications (SMS/Email).

---

## 📈 Future Expansion
*   **Pots & Accessories Module**: Independent weight-based management.
*   **Seed Lifecycle**: Expiration and germination rate tracking.
*   **Vendor Settlement**: Automated payout management.

---

## ✅ Verification & Quality
*   **Data Integrity**: Continuous validation of care metadata for all listings.
*   **Transaction Reliability**: End-to-end testing of the checkout pipeline.
*   **Seller UX**: Streamlined onboarding and listing processes.
